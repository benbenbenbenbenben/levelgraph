**Born:** September 26, 1981, Saginaw, Michigan, USA

## Career Highlights

- 23 Grand Slam singles titles
- 14 Grand Slam doubles titles (with sister Venus)
- 4 Olympic gold medals
- Held the World No. 1 ranking for 319 weeks

## Known For

Serena revolutionized women's tennis with her powerful serve and aggressive baseline play. Her rivalry and partnership with her sister Venus became one of the most compelling narratives in sports history.

## Beyond Tennis

Serena is also a fashion designer, businesswoman, and advocate for gender and racial equality in sports. She founded Serena Ventures, investing in early-stage companies.
