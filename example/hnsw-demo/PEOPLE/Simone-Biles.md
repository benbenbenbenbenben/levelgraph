# Simone Biles

Born March 14, 1997, Columbus, Ohio.

The most decorated American gymnast in history. Period.

32 Olympic and World Championship medals combined. 7 Olympic medals (4 gold). 25 World Championship medals (19 gold). Skills named after her: the Biles on floor, the Biles II on vault.
