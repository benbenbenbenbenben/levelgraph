# Venus Williams

**Born:** June 17, 1980, Lynwood, California, USA

A tennis legend and pioneer who broke barriers in professional tennis. Venus Williams paved the way for a generation of power players and remains an icon of athleticism and grace.

## Achievements

Seven-time Grand Slam singles champion. Four Olympic gold medals. Former World No. 1. Venus and her sister Serena transformed women's tennis, bringing unprecedented power and athleticism to the sport.

## Entrepreneurship

Founded V Starr Interiors, an interior design firm. Also co-owns the Miami Dolphins as part of a limited partnership. An advocate for equal prize money in tennis—her efforts helped Wimbledon achieve pay parity in 2007.

## Health Advocacy

Openly discussed her Sjögren's syndrome diagnosis, helping raise awareness about autoimmune diseases. Continues to compete at the highest levels despite health challenges.
