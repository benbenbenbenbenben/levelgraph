# Pelé

**October 23, 1940 – December 29, 2022**

Edson Arantes do Nascimento, known to the world as Pelé, is widely regarded as the greatest football (soccer) player of all time. The Brazilian forward won three FIFA World Cups—the only player to achieve this feat.

Born in Três Corações, Brazil. Grew up in poverty. Joined Santos FC at 15, where he would play for nearly two decades.

Career statistics: over 1,000 goals. Scored 77 goals for the Brazilian national team in 92 appearances.

Named "Athlete of the Century" by the International Olympic Committee. Honorary citizen of multiple countries. Ambassador for football worldwide. His name became synonymous with excellence.
