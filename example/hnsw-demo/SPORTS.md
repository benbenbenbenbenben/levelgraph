# Sports

A diverse collection of athletic pursuits and competitive activities.

## Team Sports

- **Basketball** - Fast-paced court game with five players per team
- **Soccer/Football** - The world's most popular sport
- **Ice Hockey** - Intense action on ice with sticks and pucks
- **Baseball** - America's pastime
- **American Football** - Strategic gridiron battle
- **Rugby** - Rough-and-tumble ball carrying game
- **Volleyball** - Net sport played by teams of six
- **Cricket** - Bat-and-ball game popular across the Commonwealth

## Individual Sports

- **Tennis** - Racquet sport played on various surfaces
- **Golf** - Precision club-and-ball game across 18 holes
- **Swimming** - Racing through water using various strokes
- **Track and Field** - Running, jumping, and throwing events
- **Gymnastics** - Artistic displays of strength and flexibility
- **Boxing** - Combat sport in a ring
- **Wrestling** - Grappling combat sport
- **Figure Skating** - Artistic performance on ice

## Endurance Sports

- **Marathon Running** - 26.2 miles of determination
- **Triathlon** - Swimming, cycling, and running combined
- **Cycling** - Road racing, mountain biking, and track
- **Cross-Country Skiing** - Winter endurance racing

## Racquet Sports

- **Tennis** - Grand Slams, clay, grass, and hardcourts
- **Badminton** - Fastest racquet sport
- **Table Tennis** - Lightning-quick reflexes
- **Squash** - High-intensity court game
