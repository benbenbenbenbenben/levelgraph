# Careers

Paths people take to build their professional lives.

## Technology

- **Software Engineer** - Building applications and systems
- **Data Scientist** - Finding patterns in information
- **Product Manager** - Guiding product development
- **UX Designer** - Creating user experiences
- **Systems Administrator** - Keeping infrastructure running
- **Security Analyst** - Protecting digital assets

## Healthcare

- **Doctor** - Diagnosing and treating patients
- **Nurse** - Patient care and medical support
- **Pharmacist** - Medication expertise
- **Physical Therapist** - Rehabilitation specialist
- **Surgeon** - Operating room specialist

## Education

- **Teacher** - Inspiring the next generation
- **Professor** - University-level instruction and research
- **School Administrator** - Educational leadership
- **Librarian** - Knowledge organization and access

## Business

- **Entrepreneur** - Starting and running businesses
- **Accountant** - Financial record keeping
- **Marketing Manager** - Promoting products and services
- **Human Resources** - Managing people and culture
- **Consultant** - Expert advice across industries

## Creative

- **Graphic Designer** - Visual communication
- **Architect** - Designing buildings and spaces
- **Writer** - Creating content and stories
- **Musician** - Performing and composing
- **Film Director** - Visual storytelling

## Science

- **Research Scientist** - Advancing human knowledge
- **Engineer** - Designing and building solutions
- **Biologist** - Studying living organisms
- **Chemist** - Understanding matter at molecular level
- **Physicist** - Fundamental laws of the universe

## Public Service

- **Lawyer** - Legal representation and counsel
- **Politician** - Public policy and governance
- **Social Worker** - Community support and advocacy
- **Police Officer** - Law enforcement
- **Firefighter** - Emergency response
